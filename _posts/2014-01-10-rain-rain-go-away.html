A Little--or a Lot--of Rain Won't Stop Us!

The Polar Vortex and Snowpocalypse

may have chilled our toes and given us a few days rest, but the upcoming rains won't stop us from setting up shop under the Hoop House at Normandy Farms.

Our local farmers and vendors are still offering the best in local chemical-free meats, treats, eggs, veggies and products.

See you all in the Hoop House from

9 a.m to 2 p.m.!

The old historic barns at Normandy Farms are enjoying new life with the Farm to Fork Market.
  

Vendor Listing
  
*Ann Crochets - Original crocheted creations by Ann Kelley. Sales benefit Indy's Sheltie Rescue organization.

*Bluffwood Creek Co-Op - Honeysticks, honey granola, honey cookies, kernel popcorn, popped popcorn, honey popcorn. England's Honey and Harvest Caf Coffee Company's whole beans and ground coffee. Text HONEY to 42828 to join bee farm newsletter.

Borre Brothers' Famous Fermented Vegetables - The finest, most delicious organic lacto-fermented vegetables carefully processed and fermented at home in German crocks by a band of young brothers, committed to the health and wellness of themselves and others.

*Bridgeton Mill - Organic stone ground wheat, corn, and spelt flours, ground purple corn, breakfast grits, and mixes such as pancake mix and cornbread mix.

* Countryside Orchard - Chemical free apples and raw apple cider.

*Farming Engineers -

Chicken without hormones.

*Full Circle Farm -

Pastured and Organically-fed Pork, Organically-fed pasture-raised chicken; whole chickens and breasts. We raise slow growing French hybrid and heritage breed chickens. The taste is worth the wait! Betsy's Kitchen always has special paleo baked goods!

*Friends of the Farm to Fork Market --

Stop by to sign up for our informative news blasts and to see how your volunteer time can help our new market become even better.

*LuvURCoconuts - Danielle's family members will keep her business going in her honor.

* Pet Lover's Organic Bakery - Pet treats made with all-organic ingredients, including peanut butter Barky bars, green bean gourmet treats, and sweet potato delight and chews, Scrumptious Salmon Kitty Treats, local elk antlers and catnip! Gluten/grain free treats. Sizeable percentage of proceeds donated to the Humane Society. Fall apple and peanut butter dog treats, and their new hand-crafted feather kitty toys.

*Redwine Family Farms-- Farm Fresh Chicken Eggs. Eight kinds of Hot Sauce and Pepper Seasoning. Organic Dried Herbs (over 35 kinds) and Herb Mix (OVER 24 HERBS). Spaghetti Squash. * Simple Taste - Gluten and dairy-free baked goods made in an FDA approved facility. More new recipes all the time.
  
* Stillhaven Farms -

Fresh Produce and Goat Meat.
