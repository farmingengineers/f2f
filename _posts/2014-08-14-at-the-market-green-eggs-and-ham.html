CLEAN Eggs & Ham

He looked for food that had no junk,

'Cause Red Dye put him in a funk.

There was no clean food at this store,

He did not go there anymore.

He looked for clean eggs on a plane,

Then hopped aboard a gravy train.

The next town had a hungry fox,

And all the ham came in a box.

The drive-thru only had McFood,

Which put him in a fouler mood.

The clean food was not here nor there,

He could not find it anywhere.

But then he came upon a mouse,

Who had some clean food at her house!

She said she got it from the Farm,

A place with food that did no harm.

They drove to get there in a car,

It was not even very far.

Oh yes, there were clean eggs and ham,

And yellow beans and kale with jam.

He bought some eggs and then some ham,

He even bought the kale with jam!

He did not need to shop the store,

Or use the drive-thru anymore.

His funk was gone, his mood was good

He owed it all to cleaner food.

He'll always have clean eggs and ham,

Thanks to a mouse named Sam-I-Am!

--With apologies to the late great Dr. Seuss, and in honor of all kids and teachers heading back to school this month.

See you under the shade trees Friday for market from 4-7 PM.

~Market Master Maria Smietana
