Supports People's Climate March

Jesse Kirkham from the Heartland Chapter of the Sierra Club will be in attendance at the market the next few weeks to encourage you to join with thousands of others in New York for the People's Climate March in September. A bus is ready to take you for the historic march to let world leaders know how important this issue is to us. Bring your questions to Jesse and sign up. More info is available here and here.

See you under the shade trees Friday for market from 4-7 PM.

Maria is sifting sand on Lake Michigan and will be back at the market next week.

~Market Master Maria Smietana

  
Vendor Listing
  

*3 in1 Restaurant

*Black Heart Coffee

A socially conscious cold brew coffee company looking to foster a sense of community, honor the environment, and bring delicious cold brew coffee to tables everywhere. We will be selling cold brew coffee concentrate and coffee syrup, made with fair trade organic ingredients.

*Bluffwood Creek Co-Op

Certified Naturally Grown (CNG) Honey typically harvested within a week of sale. Honey granola, honey cookies, honey-coated popcorn, honey sticks (yes, we found some stashed away). Fresh popped corn & USDA Certified Organic kernels. Certified Organic Hay for sale: alfalfa/clover/grass - square and round 4X4 bales. Text HONEY to 42828 to join bee farm newsletter.

* Borre Brothers' Famous Fermented Vegetables Organic lacto-fermented vegetables fermented in German crocks by a band of young brothers committed to the health and wellness of themselves and others. We'll have root kraut, white kraut, red kraut, GAPS beans, dilly carrots, kombucha and water kefir.

*Countryside Orchard

Countryside Orchard will have Apples - Monarch & Williams Pride. Tomatoes - Early Girl and heirloom Brandy Boy. Potatoes - red, yellow & white. Green Bell peppers. Asian eggplant. Edamame. Bi-color sweet corn. Sweet onions - white, yellow & red. Cooking onions, good keepers. Beets. Broccoli.
