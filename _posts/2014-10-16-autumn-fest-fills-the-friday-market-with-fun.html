Vendor Listing

  

Black Heart Coffee

A socially conscious cold brew coffee company looking to foster a sense of community, honor the environment, and bring delicious cold brew coffee to tables everywhere. We will be selling cold brew coffee concentrate and coffee syrup, made with fair trade organic ingredients.

Bluffwood Creek Co-Op

Rob Green is now virtually out of honey, and invites Kim England, also Certified Naturally Grown to sell honey. Bluffwood Creek Honey granola, honey cookies, honey-coated popcorn, and USDA Certified Organic popcorn is still available. Text HONEY to 42828 to join bee farm newsletter.

Borre Brothers' Famous Fermented Vegetables Back Next WeekOrganic lacto-fermented vegetables fermented in German crocks by a band of young brothers committed to the health and wellness of themselves and others. We'll have root kraut, white kraut, beets, dilly carrots, Jake's Hot Jalapenos, kombucha and kimchi.

Dream Girl Soapery

Handcrafted soaps made with organic oils and natural ingredients. Black Magic Kitchen Soap, Vetiver Soleseife, Coconut Soleseife, Green Tea & Bergamot Soleseife, Fig & Thyme Soleseife. Also Sensual, Lemon Drop, Fresh Peach, Creamy Buttermilk, Banana Leaf Acai. New: Dirty Mimosa,Herb Garden, Coconut Cassis

Farming Engineers

Whole chicken and chicken cuts are available. Also broccoli, Napa cabbage, butternut and acorn squashes, pie pumpkins, daikon and watermelon radishes, and lettuce.

*Friends of the Farm to Fork Market

Shitake Mushrooms Certified Naturally Grown from Shamrock Farm and non-GMO seeds from Urban Farmer are for sale to support efforts of the Market. Sign up for our informative e-newsletter.

Full Circle Farm Back Oct. 24

We are taking a vacation and will be back at the market on October 24. If you need something before then you can stop by the farm Monday night October 20th after 5 p.m. We would love to sell to you. Pastured Organic Heritage Chicken. Pastured organic pork. Also Guinea Girl body products made at Full Circle Farm. Come smell the new Holiday Spice lotion, and check out all the salves, lotions, butters and balms.

*Magnolia Mill

Eggplant, cucumbers, onions, green beans, cabbage, kale, potatoes, tomatoes, zucchini.

Pet Lover's Organic Bakery

Pet treats made with all-organic ingredients, including peanut butter Barky bars, green bean gourmet treats, and sweet potato delight and chews, Salmon Kitty Treats, local elk antlers and catnip! Gluten/grain free treats. Sizeable percentage of proceeds donated to the Humane Society. We still have a limited supply of Alaskan salmon dog treats.

*Redwine Family Farms

Pumpkins! Okra, hot and sweet peppers, tomatoes. Butternut, spaghetti and acorn squash. Pickling cucumbers, zucchini, yellow squash, yellow wax beans, candy onions and beets. Red Russian kale, Siberian kale, collard greens, Swiss chard, Eight kinds of hot sauce and pepper seasoning. Organic dried herbs (60 kinds) and Herb Mix (OVER 30 Herbs!)

Simple Taste Gluten and dairy-free baked goods made in an FDA approved facility. More new recipes all the time.

*The Cracked Pot

Organically-grown houseplants and garden ornamentals, including cacti and other succulents. Bedding plants in season, and water-loving plants for bird baths and garden ponds. Organic potting mix.

The Yegerlehner Family

Ground lamb, ground beef and fresh mozzi are back! Unique cheeses including Flora, Sand Crest Jack, and Jegerlehner Swiss, 100% grass-fed beef, veal, lamb. Pet products available including milk, butter, kefir, cottage cheese, whey, colostrum, buttermilk, cheese curds and raw mozzi string cheese and fresh mozzi. To reserve milk call 812-939-3027, 812-939-2813 or email

swissconnectioncheese@gmail.com.

U-Relish Farm

A variety of healthy pea, bean, and lentil-based slow-cooker ready meals-in-a-bag. Also, dips and dressing packets made from local, naturally grown herbs. Featuring all-beef HOT DOGS!

Valentine Hill Farm

Swiss Chard. 100% whole grain organic spelt English muffins and pizza crusts. More flavorful with less tannin than hard red winter wheat and a stronger nutty flavor that leaves a slightly sweet aftertaste. PLUS: A variety of whole grain breads, English muffins, French baguettes, European style lemon pound cakes, ready-to-top pizza crusts, whole wheat and pumpkin spice waffle/pancake mix and European spritz cookies. Back this week whole wheat pizza crusts and whole wheat flax seed crackers!
