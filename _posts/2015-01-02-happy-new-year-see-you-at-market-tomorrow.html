This Saturday: Start Your Year with Your Farm Friends

Remember last year at this time? We had to start every market with snow removal, walkway salting, and ice chopping for what seemed like weeks. I know there is a lot of winter left yet, but I'm a glass-half-full kinda gal, so I'm grateful that we've made it this far without a major snow dump.

If the holiday season left your digestive system in turmoil and added pounds you didn't ask for, add some fermented beverages and foods to your diet. The Yegerlehners have a nice selecton of kombucha at their table, along with their usual line-up of delicious dairy products and meats.

Valentine Hill Farm finally got a winter-worthy hoophouse built this year, and are still picking Swiss Chard that was planted back in July! Come get a bundle to add to your salads or stir fries.

See Full Circle Farm for poultry and organic skin care products. While you're there, ask Paul how the chicken rodeo went. He and Betsy took in a nice flock of heritage breed chickens from a local farmer who's giving up raising poultry, so that means more eggs once the days start getting longer!

Bluffwood Creek has a limited supply of eggs. As always, come early for best selection.

~Market Master Maria Smietana
