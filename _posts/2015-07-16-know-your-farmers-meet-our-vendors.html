Vendor Listing

Barnyard Basics

Natural and eco-friendly products for home. New: a liquid version of our laundry detergent, liquid fabric softener, dishwasher detergent and rinse aid. We also have powdered laundry detergent, wool dryer balls, un-paper towels, and

sandwich bags. Stop by and check out all of our natural, eco-friendly cleaners and reusable rather than disposable items for your home.

Blue Yonder Organic Farm

A 43 acre diversified farm committed to producing healthy organic food, produced exclusively by military veterans. The farm produces a wide variety of fresh vegetables, fruits, meats, eggs, and honey using organic methods. These products are sold under the label of Homegrown by Heroes, a national certification designating agricultural products grown by military veterans. Blue Yonder Organic Farm will have chicken, raspberries and eggs at market.

Bluffwood Creek Co-Op

Our CNG Certification now includes honey, poultry and eggs. CNG mirrors and sometimes exceeds USDA Organic standards, but, in our opinion, has better anti-cruelty standards. It's best to reserve eggs. Frozen chickens, 2-4 lbs. available, and limited turkeys for Thanksgiving. Reserve with your name and items (turkey, chickens or eggs) by texting 317.516.6493 or email r.green@techsupportdept.net. We have a great new cookie--honey shortbread, plus honey, beeswax hand cream, honey caramel corn, popping corn, honey cookies, cornmeal and granola. Text HONEY to 42828 to join bee farm newsletter.

Farming Engineers

Heirloom, slicing, and cherry tomatoes, garlic, beans, chard, summer squash, zucchini, green onions.

Friends of the Farm to Fork Market

Sign up for our informative e-newsletter, and use our convenient credit card service if you prefer not to carry cash. Quinoa and Amaranth Granola mixes are back at the Friends' booth.

Full Circle Farm

A limited supply of pastured organically fed pork available; more cuts this week. Free-range, organically fed chicken and eggs also available. We raise heritage, slow-growing chicken, (whole chicken and cuts); you can taste the difference. Grass-fed beef summer sausage without any nitrates is also available. Don't forget your skin - your body's biggest organ. Just in time for the heat, Guinea Girl has sunscreen sticks without the harsh chemicals. Also body lotions, butter, powders and salves.

Harvest Cafe Coffee Company A variety of locally-roasted, Certified Organic coffees from around the globe. Choose either whole beans or ground.

Magnolia Mills

Green beans, onions, potatoes, cucumbers, yellow and green squash, lettuce, kale, cabbage, green tomatoes, maybe more!

Nature Cycle Household cleaning products made with natural ingredients. Safe for people, pets, and the environment. This week, we will have our all-purpose cleaner, glass cleaner, and scouring powder.

Pet Lover's Organic Bakery

Pet treats made with all-organic ingredients, including peanut butter Barky Bars, green bean gourmet treats, and sweet potato delight and chews, gluten/grain free treats. Sizeable percentage of proceeds donated to the Humane Society.

pH3rment Decorative house plants and garden bedding plants will be at the market this week. Fermented vegetables. A variety of flavorful krauts, vegetables and Kimchi. What's good for your gut is good for the rest of you! Enjoy our

Caraway Dill Kraut, Kimchi and Beets.

The Yegerlehner Family

We will have some of this spring's Jack and Colby, and Garlic and Herb as well as the standbys of Swiss, Flora, Beit and Pazia. Also will have a limited amount of fresh Mozz. Plus: milk, butter, buttermilk, cottage cheese, country style farmers cheese, whey, kefir, cheese curds and colostrum are available. Also, a variety of natural rind cheeses and more to come later. Beef, veal and some lamb cuts available too. Please call or email by 8:00 Friday morning for milk orders; 812-939-3027 or swissconnectioncheese@gmail.com
